ABOUT

Exercise has always been a big part of my life – I originally trained as a dancer before qualifying as a Personal Trainer and Fitness Instructor. I believe that keeping fit helps you not just physically but mentally too and I'm passionate about sharing my knowledge.

I love working with people at the beginning of their journey, as well as those with experience. I pride myself on making my clients feel comfortable, happy and motivated. My main aim is to help you achieve your goals.

Let's start your journey today!

PERSONAL TRAINING

With personal training, we'll work on a programme tailored to you. We'll build on your unique strengths and weaknesses to get the results you want.

APP-BASED TRAINING

I also provide online and app-based training designed around you and your lifestyle. Log in to find out your workout each day and I'll be there to guide and support you all the way.

It's perfect if you want help staying on track between sessions, feel uncomfortable in the gym or want to be more flexible with where you work out.
